About

Hello there! My name is Karla and I love creating awesome things with code. I’m a self-taught (self-guided? self-driven?) web developer with a passion for learning, being creative, and making impactful experiences for the web. Tech is a huge part of modern life and in my experince there is a lot of room for improvement. My goal is to be part of the solution by building websites that are delightful and created with accessibility in mind.

Although I consider myself a front-end developer, I also enjoy web and UX design and love the challenge of designing my own projects. As someone who is making a career change into tech, I’m excited to be a part of a community that gets to solve challenging problems with creative solutions that will impact so many people!

I’m currently seeking out my first role as a front-end developer in an (ideally) remote position. If you’re interested, please reach out at karla.walker@hey.com.
